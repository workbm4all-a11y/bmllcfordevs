Privacy Policy - BoardMate
1. Introduction

Welcome to BoardMate, a private EdTech platform created to help Bihar Board students (Classes 9–12) learn, practice, and test their knowledge online. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect
  • Basic details such as your name, email, and class (only when you register).
  • Usage data like quiz attempts, scores, and time spent for learning improvement.
  • We use third-party advertising partners (e.g., Google AdSense) to serve relevant ads.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide a better learning experience, personalize quizzes, and show relevant content and ads. BoardMate does not sell or share personal data with any unrelated third parties.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to save your preferences and enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Advertisements

Our platform is ad-supported. Ads help us offer free educational content to all users. Third-party ad services may use cookies to display personalized ads based on your browsing patterns.

6. Data Security

We employ security practices to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, online systems can never be 100% secure, so users share information at their own discretion.

7. Children’s Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of minors. Students under 18 are encouraged to use the platform under the guidance of parents or guardians.

8. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The most recent version will always be available on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns, contact us at contactboardmate@gmail.com/contact.boardmate@protonmail.com .
